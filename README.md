# endowment-asset-allocation
1. Purpose

The purpose of this portfolio is to simulate an institutional endowment-style fund designed to support long-term capital growth while maintaining sustainable annual distributions. The portfolio emphasizes intergenerational equity and purchasing power preservation.


2. Investment Objective

The portfolio seeks to achieve a long-term annualized return of:

7.50% = 4.25% Spending + 0.75% Expenses + 2.50% Inflation

Performance is evaluated over rolling 5–10 year periods.


3. Time Horizon

The portfolio has a perpetual investment horizon with low short-term liquidity needs. Short-term volatility is acceptable in pursuit of long-term real growth.


4. Risk Tolerance

The portfolio maintains a moderate-to-high risk tolerance, driven primarily by equity exposure. Diversification across global equities, fixed income, and real assets mitigates concentration risk.

6. Rebalancing Policy

Portfolio reviewed quarterly

Rebalancing triggered if allocation breaches policy range

Cash flows used first to minimize transaction costs

Trades executed to return allocation within approved bands


7. Spending Policy

Annual spending equals 4.25% of trailing 48-month average portfolio value to reduce volatility impact and maintain sustainability.


8. Permitted Investments

Publicly traded ETFs representing broad asset classes

Investment-grade fixed income

Real assets (REITs & infrastructure)

No speculative leverage or derivatives
